Kenyan Beef Stew Recipe

Description

Kenyan beef stew is hearty dish featuring tender, slow-cooked beef simmered in a rich, savory broth with a variety of vegetables like potatoes, carrots, onions, and peas. For every cook, there is a slightly different version of beef stew, and the recipe below is no different. Feel free to modify the recipe below at will.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 tbsp garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp curry powder
  • 500 gms beef, cubed
  • 1 beef stock cube
  • 3 tomatoes, diced
  • 4 cups water
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Salt to taste
  • Coriander leaves, chopped

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a heavy based pot.
  2. Add the onions and stir. Cook for 30 seconds or until translucent.
  3. Stir in the garlic and curry powder.
  4. Add the beef cubes, stock cube, salt, pepper, tomatoes and water and stir.
  5. Cover the pot with a lid and let simmer for 30-40 minutes.
  6. Garnish with coriander leaves and enjoy, ideally with chapati!
